# Power Armor

**Type:** Mechanized infantry combat armor  
**Manufacturer:** Various (West-Tek, US Military, etc.)  
**Users:** [[Brotherhood of Steel]], Enclave, military

## Overview
Power Armor is an advanced pre-war military technology consisting of mechanized, powered exoskeletons that turn soldiers into walking tanks. Developed for the Resource Wars and perfected for the Chinese theater, Power Armor became one of [[The Fallout Universe]]'s most iconic and powerful technologies. Post-war, it's primarily associated with the [[Brotherhood of Steel]].
