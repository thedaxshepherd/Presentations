# Fallout 3

**Release Date:** October 28, 2008  
**Developer:** Bethesda Game Studios  
**Setting:** Capital Wasteland (Washington D.C.)  
**Protagonist:** Lone Wanderer

## Overview
Fallout 3 marked Bethesda's first entry in the Fallout series and successfully transitioned it to first-person 3D gameplay. Set in the ruins of Washington D.C., the game follows the Lone Wanderer's search for their father and quest to bring clean water to the wasteland.

## Setting
- **Capital Wasteland:** Devastated Washington D.C. and surroundings
- 200 years after [[The Great War]]
- Notable landmarks: Capitol Building, Lincoln Memorial, Washington Monument (all in ruins)
- Super Mutant occupation
- [[Brotherhood of Steel]] presence

## Main Story
1. Born in [[Vault 101]]
2. Childhood and coming of age in Vault
3. Father James escapes, forcing player to leave
4. Search for father in wasteland
5. Project Purity: Bring clean water to Capital Wasteland
6. Conflict with [[Enclave]]
7. Final sacrifice or delegate (depending on DLC)

## Gameplay
- Real-time combat with VATS (Vault-Tec Assisted Targeting System)
- Pip-Boy 3000 interface
- S.P.E.C.I.A.L. character system
- Karma system (Good/Neutral/Evil)
- Exploration of ruins and vaults

## Key Characters
- **Lone Wanderer:** Player character
- **James:** Father, scientist
- **Three Dog:** Radio DJ at Galaxy News Radio
- **Elder Lyons:** [[Brotherhood of Steel]] leader
- **President Eden:** [[Enclave]] AI leader
- **Colonel Autumn:** Enclave military leader

## Factions
- [[Brotherhood of Steel]] (Elder Lyons chapter)
- [[Enclave]] - Main antagonists
- Talon Company - Mercenaries
- Paradise Falls - Slavers
- Various settlements (Megaton, Rivet City, Tenpenny Tower)

## Notable Locations
- [[Vault 101]] - Starting location
- Megaton - Settlement built around unexploded bomb
- Rivet City - Aircraft carrier settlement
- The Citadel - Brotherhood of Steel HQ
- Vault 87 - Super Mutant origin

## DLC Expansions
1. **Operation: Anchorage** - Pre-war simulation
2. **The Pitt** - Industrial wasteland
3. **Broken Steel** - Continues after main story
4. **Point Lookout** - Swampland
5. **Mothership Zeta** - Alien abduction

## Impact
- Revitalized Fallout franchise
- Established modern Fallout gameplay formula
- Won numerous Game of the Year awards
- Introduced millions to [[Fallout Universe]]

## Themes
- Father-child relationship
- Sacrifice for greater good
- Hope in desolation
- Rebuilding civilization
- Moral choices matter
