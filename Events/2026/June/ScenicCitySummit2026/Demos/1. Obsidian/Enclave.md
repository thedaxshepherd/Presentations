# Enclave

**Type:** Remnant of U.S. government  
**Founded:** Pre-[[The Great War]]  
**Goal:** Restore pre-war America (their vision)  
**Symbol:** Stylized "E" shield

## Overview
The Enclave is a secretive organization claiming to be the continuation of the pre-war United States government. Composed of high-ranking government officials, military officers, and wealthy elites who survived [[The Great War]] in secure locations, they view themselves as humanity's rightful rulers.

## Origins
- Pre-war shadow government
- Oil rig installation off California
- Survived war intact
- Preserved advanced technology
- Maintained chain of command

## Ideology
- Pure strain humanity only
- Wastelanders are "mutants"
- Restore America under their control
- Technological superiority
- Authoritarian rule

## Technology
- **Advanced Power Armor** (Enclave variant)
- **Vertibirds** - Military aircraft
- **Plasma weapons**
- **Energy shields**
- Pre-war military tech
