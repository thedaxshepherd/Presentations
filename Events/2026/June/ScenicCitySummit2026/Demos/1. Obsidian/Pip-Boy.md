# Pip-Boy

**Full Name:** Personal Information Processor - Boy  
**Manufacturer:** RobCo Industries  
**Type:** Wrist-mounted computer  
**Users:** Vault dwellers primarily

## Overview
The Pip-Boy is an iconic wrist-mounted personal computer created by RobCo Industries before [[The Great War]]. Standard issue to all [[Vault-Tec Corporation]] vault residents, it serves as a combination health monitor, inventory manager, map system, and radio. The Pip-Boy is one of the most recognizable symbols of [[The Fallout Universe]].
