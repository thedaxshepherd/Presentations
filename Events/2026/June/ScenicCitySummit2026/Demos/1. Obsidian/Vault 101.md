# Vault 101

**Location:** Capital Wasteland (Washington D.C.)  
**Game:** [[Fallout 3]]  
**Experiment:** Never meant to open  
**Motto:** "Vault 101: Jewel of the Wastes"

## The Experiment
[[Vault-Tec]]'s experiment was to keep Vault 101 permanently sealed, testing how an isolated community would develop over generations without any contact with the outside world.

## Key Events
- Sealed during [[The Great War]] (2077)
- Remained closed for 200 years
- James (Lone Wanderer's father) escapes (2277)
- Lone Wanderer forced to leave
- First breach in Vault's history

## Notable Residents
- **Lone Wanderer** - Player character
- **James** - Scientist father
- **Overseer Almodovar** - Authoritarian leader
- **Amata** - Overseer's daughter, friend to player

## Locations
- Living quarters
- Overseer's office
- Reactor room
- Atrium (central gathering)

## The Crisis
James's escape creates chaos and forces the Lone Wanderer to flee. Player can return later to find Vault in political turmoil.

## Gameplay Role
- Tutorial area for [[Fallout 3]]
- Character creation
- Childhood sequence
- Introduction to Pip-Boy
