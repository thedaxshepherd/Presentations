# Fallout 76

**Release Date:** November 14, 2018
**Developer:** Bethesda Game Studios
**Setting:** Appalachia (West Virginia)
**Protagonist:** Vault Dweller (player-created)

## Overview
Fallout 76 is Bethesda's first online multiplayer Fallout game, set in the hills of post-apocalyptic West Virginia. Players emerge from [[Vault 76]] on Reclamation Day — just 25 years after [[The Great War]] — to reclaim and rebuild Appalachia. The world is emptied of human NPCs at launch, though later updates added them back.

## Setting
- **Appalachia:** West Virginia, relatively untouched by nuclear devastation
- Only 25 years after [[The Great War]] — the earliest in the main series timeline
- Notable regions: The Forest, Toxic Valley, Ash Heap, Savage Divide, Cranberry Bog, The Mire
- New threat: Scorchbeasts and the Scorched plague

## Main Story
1. Exit [[Vault 76]] on Reclamation Day (October 27, 2102)
2. Follow the Overseer's holotape trail across Appalachia
3. Discover what happened to Appalachia's survivors
4. Confront the Scorched plague threatening the region
5. Launch nuclear missiles to create blast zones for rare resources
6. Defeat the Scorchbeast Queen
7. Begin the true reclamation of Appalachia

## Factions (Pre-War / Historical)
- **Free States** - Survivalists who distrusted the government
- **Responders** - Emergency workers who tried to help survivors
- **Raiders** - Violent survivors (later redeemed in Wastelanders DLC)
- **Enclave** - Government remnants with nuclear codes
- **Brotherhood of Steel** - First Expeditionary Force arrives later (Steel Dawn DLC)

## Gameplay Innovations
- First fully online multiplayer Fallout
- C.A.M.P. system for building and placing a mobile base
- Nuclear launch mechanic (find codes, launch missiles, create high-level zones)
- Perk card system replaces traditional perks
- No human NPCs at launch (added in Wastelanders, 2020)
- Live service model with seasonal updates

## Key Updates & DLC
1. **Wastelanders (2020)** - Added human NPCs, factions (Raiders vs. Settlers), and a full story
2. **Steel Dawn / Steel Reign** - Brotherhood of Steel arrives in Appalachia
3. **The Pitt** - Expeditions to a new location
4. **Atlantic City** - Further expeditions content
5. **Skyline Valley** - Expanded map and new story

## Notable Characters (Post-Wastelanders)
- **The Overseer** - Leaves Vault 76 early, guides players via holotapes
- **Rose** - Raider robot, leader of the Top of the World
- **Sophia Daguerre** - Astronaut survivor with a mysterious past
- **Beckett** - Raider-turned-companion
- **Penelope Hornwright** - Pre-war industrialist's daughter

## Notable Locations
- [[Vault 76]] - Starting location
- Flatwoods - First settlement encountered
- Charleston - Flooded state capital
- Top of the World - Ski resort occupied by Raiders
- The Whitespring Resort - Upscale pre-war hotel
- Mothman Museum - Point Pleasant landmark

## Themes
- Hope and a new beginning (earliest post-war timeline)
- Community and cooperation (multiplayer)
- The consequences of nuclear escalation
- Rebuilding from scratch
- What it means to be human without society
