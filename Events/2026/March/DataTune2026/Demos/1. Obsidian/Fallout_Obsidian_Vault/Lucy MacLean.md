# Lucy MacLean

**Played by:** Ella Purnell  
**Home:** [[Vault 33]]  
**Role:** Protagonist of [[Fallout TV Series]]

## Background
Lucy grew up in [[Vault 33]], a seemingly perfect underground society. Raised with pre-war American values and optimism, she represents the idealized Vault dweller - polite, eager to help, and believing in the best of humanity.

## Character Traits
- **Optimistic:** Believes in goodness of people
- **Naive:** Sheltered from wasteland realities
- **Determined:** Won't give up on mission
- **Resourceful:** Adapts to surface challenges
- **Idealistic:** Clings to Vault values

## Story Arc
1. Comfortable life in [[Vault 33]]
2. Father [[Hank MacLean]] kidnapped (or escapes?)
3. Volunteers to find him on surface
4. First contact with brutal wasteland
5. Teams up with [[Maximus]] and encounters [[The Ghoul]]
6. Discovers dark truths about [[Vault-Tec]]
7. Questions everything she was taught

## Equipment
- Vault 33 jumpsuit (blue and yellow)
- Pip-Boy on wrist
- Tranq gun (non-lethal preference)
- Eventually acquires wasteland weapons
- Maintains Vault appearance

## Relationships

### [[Hank MacLean]]
- Father, Overseer of Vault 33
- Searching for him drives plot
- May not be who she thinks

### [[Maximus]]
- Brotherhood squire she meets
- Unlikely alliance
- Potential romance
- Teaches her wasteland survival

### [[The Ghoul]]
- Antagonistic bounty hunter
- Captures her multiple times
- Represents wasteland cynicism
- Challenges her worldview

## Character Development
- Starts naive, becomes hardened
- Learns wasteland is not black and white
- Moral challenges test Vault values
- Must choose between ideology and survival
- Discovers [[Vault-Tec]] secrets

## Themes
- Innocence lost
- Clash of Vault culture vs wasteland reality
- Hope vs cynicism
- Truth about utopia
- Coming of age in apocalypse

## Contrast to Wasteland
- Clean vs dirty
- Optimistic vs cynical
- Trusting vs paranoid
- Idealistic vs pragmatic
- Pre-war values vs survival

## Season 1 Journey
- Leaves [[Vault 33]] for first time
- Horrified by surface violence
- Forced to make impossible choices
- Learns to fight and survive
- Uncovers conspiracy about Vaults
