# ☢️ Fallout Faction Relationship Map

This diagram maps out the relationships between the major factions, species, and forces in the Fallout universe — including how Vault-Tec connects everything.

## Reading the Map

The diagram shows how each major faction relates to the others across all Fallout games and the TV series. Vault-Tec sits at the top as the common origin point — their secret experiments created the conditions for every other faction to emerge.

**Key relationships to note:**
- **Vault-Tec → Enclave**: The Enclave is essentially the surviving U.S. government that Vault-Tec was intertwined with
- **Vault-Tec → The Institute**: Vault-Tec's research funding created the preconditions for MIT's underground Institute
- **Brotherhood of Steel vs. Everyone**: The Brotherhood is in conflict with the Enclave, Institute, Super Mutants, and views Ghouls with hostility
- **NCR**: The most democratic faction, protecting Vault Dwellers and rivaling the Brotherhood
