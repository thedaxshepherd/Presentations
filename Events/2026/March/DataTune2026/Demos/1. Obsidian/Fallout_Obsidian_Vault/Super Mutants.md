# Super Mutants

**Type:** FEV-created humanoids  
**Height:** 7-12 feet  
**Created by:** Forced Evolutionary Virus (FEV)  
**Intelligence:** Varies wildly

## Overview
Super Mutants are enormous, immensely strong humanoids created by exposure to the Forced Evolutionary Virus (FEV). Unlike [[Ghouls]], which result from radiation, Super Mutants are products of deliberate pre-war military research gone wrong. They represent one of [[The Fallout Universe]]'s greatest threats and greatest tragedies.

## Origin of FEV

### Pre-War Development
- Military bio-weapon research
- Forced Evolutionary Virus
- Create super-soldiers
- West-Tek laboratories
- Mariposa Military Base
- Unethical human experimentation

### Discovery by [[Brotherhood of Steel]]
- Captain Maxson found FEV experiments
- Executed commanding officers
- Declared rebellion
- Days before [[The Great War]]
- Horror at human testing

### Post-War Release
- FEV vats survived [[The Great War]]
- Various factions found them
- Experimented with virus
- Created Super Mutants
- Multiple origins depending on region

## Creation Process

### FEV Exposure
- Humans dipped in FEV vats
- Extremely painful transformation
- Takes hours to complete
- Survival not guaranteed
- Intelligence preservation random
- Irreversible process

### Optimal Subjects
- Pure, unirradiated humans
- [[Vault 111]]-type cryo subjects ideal
- Radiation damages FEV results
- Wastelanders create less intelligent mutants
- Pre-war DNA best
- Explains vault raids
