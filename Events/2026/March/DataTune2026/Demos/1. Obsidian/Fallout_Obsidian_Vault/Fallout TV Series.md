# Fallout TV Series

**Release Date:** April 11, 2024  
**Platform:** Amazon Prime Video  
**Showrunners:** Graham Wagner, Geneva Robertson-Dworet  
**Executive Producer:** Todd Howard (Bethesda)

## Overview
The Fallout TV series is a live-action adaptation set in the [[Fallout Universe]], taking place in post-apocalyptic Los Angeles. The show runs parallel to the games, introducing new characters and stories while respecting established lore.

## Setting
- Los Angeles wasteland
- 219 years after [[The Great War]]
- New California region
- Both Vault and surface world
- Connected to game canon

## Main Characters

### [[Lucy MacLean]]
- Vault dweller from [[Vault 33]]
- Optimistic and naive
- Ventures to surface to find father
- Played by Ella Purnell
- Fish-out-of-water protagonist

### [[Maximus]]
- [[Brotherhood of Steel]] squire/knight
- Orphan raised by Brotherhood
- Conflicted about faction's methods
- Played by Aaron Moten
- Questions Brotherhood ideology

### [[The Ghoul]] (Cooper Howard)
- Pre-war movie star turned bounty hunter
- Survived 200+ years as ghoul
- Cynical and ruthless
- Played by Walton Goggins
- Connects pre-war to post-war

## Story Threads
- Lucy's search for [[Hank MacLean]] (father)
- Maximus's Brotherhood mission
- The Ghoul hunting targets
- Mystery of interconnected Vaults
- [[Vault-Tec]]'s true purposes revealed

## Vaults Featured
- [[Vault 33]] - Lucy's home, "utopian"
- Vault 31 - Connected to 33
- Vault 32 - Connected to 33
- Vault 4 - Surface dweller community
- Various ruined Vaults

## Factions
- [[Vault-Tec]] Corporation
