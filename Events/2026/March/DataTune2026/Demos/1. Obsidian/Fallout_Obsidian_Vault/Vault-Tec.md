# Vault-Tec

**Full Name:** Vault-Tec Corporation  
**Type:** Defense contractor  
**Role:** Creator of the Vault system

## Overview
Vault-Tec was a pre-war corporation that built underground survival shelters called "Vaults." While publicly marketed as humanity's salvation, Vault-Tec's true purpose was conducting social experiments on unwitting Vault dwellers, as revealed throughout the [[Fallout Universe]].

## The Vault Program

### Public Mission
- Protect citizens from nuclear war
- Preserve humanity underground
- Self-sustaining communities

### True Purpose
- Conduct unethical social experiments
- Test human behavior under extreme conditions
- Most Vaults designed with fatal flaws
- Only 17 "control" Vaults functional

## Notable Vault Experiments
- [[Vault 101]] - Never meant to open
- [[Vault 111]] - Cryogenic preservation
- [[Vault 33]] - Utopian "perfect" society (TV)
- [[Vault 13]] - Extended isolation
- [[Vault 76]] - Control Vault (functional)
- Vault 11 - Annual human sacrifice
- Vault 112 - Virtual reality
