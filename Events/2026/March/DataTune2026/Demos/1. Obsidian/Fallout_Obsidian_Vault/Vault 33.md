# Vault 33

**Location:** Los Angeles area  
**Appears In:** [[Fallout TV Series]]  
**Type:** Interconnected tri-vault system  
**Status:** Active "utopian" community

## Overview
Vault 33 is one of three interconnected Vaults (31, 32, 33) featured prominently in the [[Fallout TV Series]]. It's presented as an idyllic, functioning Vault community with seemingly happy residents who follow strict protocols.

## The Tri-Vault System
- Connected to Vault 31 and Vault 32
- Share resources and gene pool
- Exchange programs between Vaults
- Breeding program to maintain diversity
- Overseen by [[Vault-Tec]]

## Key Residents

### [[Lucy MacLean]]
- Protagonist of TV series
- Vault dweller seeking her father
- Optimistic and naive
- Ventures into wasteland
- Questions Vault's perfect facade

### [[Hank MacLean]]
- Lucy's father
- Overseer of Vault 33
- Kidnapped (apparently)
- Dark secrets

### Norm MacLean
- Lucy's brother
- More skeptical of Vault
- Investigates mysteries

## Vault Culture
- 1950s American aesthetic
- Strict social protocols
- Arranged marriages
- Community gatherings
- Propaganda and conditioning

## The Reality
- Not everything is as perfect as it seems
- Dark secrets about Vault's purpose
- Connection to surface world
- [[Vault-Tec]] experiments continue
- Truth revealed throughout series

## Surface Contact
- Raiders attack Vault
