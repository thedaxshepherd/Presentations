# The Ghoul (Cooper Howard)

**Played by:** Walton Goggins  
**Type:** Ghoul (radiation-preserved human)  
**Pre-War Name:** Cooper Howard  
**Role:** Antagonist/Anti-hero in [[Fallout TV Series]]

## Overview
The Ghoul is a 200+ year-old bounty hunter who survived [[The Great War]] and became a ghoul - a radiation-damaged human with extended lifespan. Once a famous pre-war movie star named Cooper Howard, he's now a ruthless wasteland survivor.

## Pre-War Life (Cooper Howard)
- Famous actor in 1950s-era Hollywood
- Star of Western films and [[Vault-Tec]] commercials
- Married with daughter
- Became Vault-Tec spokesperson
- Present when bombs fell (2077)

## Transformation to Ghoul
- Exposed to massive radiation during [[The Great War]]
- Skin became leathery and decayed
- Gained extended lifespan (200+ years)
- Requires anti-feral medication
- Lost humanity over centuries

## Character Traits
- **Ruthless:** Kills without hesitation
- **Cynical:** Seen worst of humanity
- **Experienced:** Survived 200+ years
- **Pragmatic:** Does whatever it takes
- **Haunted:** Memories of lost life
- **Skilled:** Master gunslinger

## Wasteland Survival
- Expert bounty hunter
- Quick draw gunfighter
- Uses pre-war and wasteland weapons
- Intimate knowledge of wasteland
- No mercy for targets

## Equipment
