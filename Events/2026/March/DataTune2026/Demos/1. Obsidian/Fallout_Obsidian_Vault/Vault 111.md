# Vault 111

**Location:** Commonwealth (Boston area)  
**Game:** [[Fallout 4]]  
**Experiment:** Cryogenic suspension  
**Status:** All residents deceased (except Sole Survivor)

## The Experiment
[[Vault-Tec]] told residents they were entering a standard Vault, but actually cryogenically froze everyone indefinitely to study long-term suspended animation.

## Timeline
- **Oct 23, 2077:** Residents frozen during [[The Great War]]
- **~2227:** [[The Institute]] operatives enter, kill spouse, kidnap Shaun
- **Oct 23, 2287:** Sole Survivor wakes (210 years later)

## The Tragedy
- All other residents died when life support failed
- Spouse murdered by mercenary Kellogg  
- Infant son Shaun kidnapped
- Used for perfect pre-war DNA by [[The Institute]]

## Notable Residents
- **Sole Survivor** - Player character, war veteran or lawyer
- **Spouse** - Murdered by Kellogg
- **Shaun** - Becomes "Father," leader of [[The Institute]]

## Locations
- Cryogenic chamber
- Overseer's office (experiment logs)
- Unused living quarters

## Gameplay Role
- Opening sequence shows pre-war life
- Tutorial area
- Witness spouse's murder while frozen
- Motivation for main quest (find Shaun)

## Themes
- Loss and grief
- Displacement in time
- Pre-war perspective
- Fish-out-of-water
