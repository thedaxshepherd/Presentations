# New California Republic

**Abbreviation:** NCR  
**Founded:** 2189  
**Capital:** Shady Sands  
**Type:** Democratic federation  
**Symbol:** Two-headed bear

## Overview
The New California Republic is the largest post-war nation in the [[Fallout Universe]], a democratic federation of towns and cities in California. Modeled on pre-war United States government, they represent an attempt to rebuild civilization with rule of law.

## Government Structure
- **President:** Elected leader
- **Congress:** Representative legislature
- **Military:** NCR Rangers and Army
- **Courts:** Legal system
- Democratic elections

## Territory
- Most of California
- Parts of Nevada, Oregon, Mexico
- Expanding eastward
- Constant border conflicts
- Administrative challenges

## Military
- **NCR Rangers:** Elite scouts and fighters
- **NCR Troopers:** Regular army
- Overstretched forces
- Pre-war weapons and armor
- Brahmin-drawn supply lines
